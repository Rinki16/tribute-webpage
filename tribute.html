KALPANA CHAWLA

Pride of India

  • Kalpana Chawla was First Indian born spacewomen
  • She was born on 17 March 1962 at Karnal in Haryana, India.
  • She completed Aeronautical Engineering in 1976 from Punjab Engineering College.
  • She also had pursued a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Colorado in 1988
  • She joined NASA in 1988
  • It was 19 November 1997 when she started her first space mission in Space Shuttle Columbia
  • In her second flight ‘STS-07’, she was a member of 7 crews of the flight.
  • In 1983, she married John Pierre Harrison, a flight instructor.
  • It was 1 February 2003 when she died when she was on a space mission.
  • NASA has developed a supercomputer in memory of the great Kalpana Chawla.
